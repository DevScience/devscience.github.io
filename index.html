Our Mission

DevScience is a nonprofit organization that teaches computer science classes to students from 5th to 8th grade. All profits that we earn are donated back to the Fremont Unified School District, for the benefit of your students. Led by students, for students.

Our Course

Introduction to Java: Object-Oriented Programming

Java is a universally recognized programming language used by millions of developers and companies across the globe. Java is a beautiful language suited for beginning programmers, allowing them to grasp the fundamentals of computer science easily. Throughout the 8 class session, students will be taught the basics of programming (variables, functions, loops, objects, etc.), and have the opportunity to create basic programs, apps, and games of their own. By the end of the session, students will be ready to tackle more advanced concepts in Java, and begin exploring the many depths of computing.
