Our Mission

DevScience is a nonprofit organization that teaches computer science classes to students from 5th to 8th grade. All profits that we earn are donated back to the Fremont Unified School District, for the benefit of your students. Led by students, for students.

Our Course

Fundamentals of Computing

Our curriculum is adapted from Stanford University's famous CS101 course, introduction to computing principles. Through the course, students will learn the basics of programming (variables, loops, if logic, etc.) through JavaScript as well as fundamental concepts in hardware and computer networking. By the end of the session, students will be prepared to tackle any programming language, as well as explore more advanced concepts in computing.
